How to Play

Mancala is a two player player game in which players take turns moving pebbles to try and place more pebbles in their scoring cup than the other player. When moving pebbles, a player can only move pebbles that are on their side of the board. When moving pebbles from one cup, the player must drop one pebble in each cup along the way to the scoring cup. If the player ends on their scoring cup, they get to take another turn. Otherwise, it is the other player's turn. When a player clears their side of the board, all remaining pebbles are placed in the other player's scoring cup. The player with the most pebbles in their scoring cup wins!
